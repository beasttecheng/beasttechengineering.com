APEX PDH is a digital continuing education company targeting licensed professionals (PEs, CPAs, attorneys, physicians, nurses, architects) who must complete mandatory Professional Development Hours (PDH) or Continuing Education Units (CEU) to maintain licensure. Unlike generic platforms, APEX PDH's niche is **AI literacy and emerging technology** — the fastest-growing and most urgently needed knowledge gap in every licensed profession.

Courses are AI-native: built with multi-agent Claude workflows, delivered via interactive artifacts, and taught by a licensed PE with direct practitioner experience. This isn't theory — it's field intelligence.

---

## THE PROBLEM

Every licensed professional in America must earn CE/PDH credits annually. Most hate it.

- **Engineers (PE):** 30 PDH/2 years in most states. Ethics required. Technology electives wide open.
- **CPAs:** 40 CPE/year. Includes technology, ethics, specialized topics.
- **Attorneys:** 12–15 CLE/year. Includes ethics and technology in many states.
- **Physicians/NPs:** 40–50 CME/2 years. AI in clinical decision-making now an approved topic.
- **Architects (AIA/NCARB):** 24 LU/2 years. Technology and sustainability emphasis.

**The gap:** Professionals earning $150k–$500k+ are spending hours on boring, checkbox compliance webinars when they desperately need to understand AI — because it's already changing their fields. Regulators are starting to require AI literacy. Courts are addressing AI-generated legal work. State engineering boards are beginning to discuss AI in stamped documents.

**The opportunity:** Be the first premium, practitioner-built AI continuing education platform for licensed professionals before every bootcamp and LinkedIn Learning course floods this space.

APEX PDH delivers:

1. **AI Literacy Courses** (1–3 PDH each) — Approved for PE, CPA, CLE, CME credit

- Launch LinkedIn presence as "PE + AI practitioner" — document real workflows

- Hire 1–2 course instructors with PE/CPA/JD credentials in adjacent areas
- Pursue IACET or NASBA accreditation for national CPA CPE approval
- Build AI-graded quiz + certificate automation via Claude API
