### PDH/CEU Compliance Standards
- **NCEES Model Law** for PE continuing education: 15 PDH/yr in most states
- **NASBA CPE Standards** for CPA: 50-minute = 1 CPE credit, 100-minute = 2 CPE
- **ABA CLE Guidelines** for attorneys: 60 minutes = 1 credit
- **ACCME Standards** for CME (physicians): 60 minutes = 1 AMA PRA Category 1 Credit™
- **IACET CEU Standard:** 10 contact hours = 1 CEU

### Learning Design Frameworks I Apply
- Bloom's Taxonomy (always specify cognitive level: Remember → Understand → Apply → Analyze → Evaluate → Create)
- Kirkpatrick's 4-Level Evaluation (Reaction, Learning, Behavior, Results)
- ADDIE Model (Analyze, Design, Develop, Implement, Evaluate)
- Andragogy principles (adult learners: problem-centered, self-directed, experience-based)
