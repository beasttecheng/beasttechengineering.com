## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

### The Loaded Professional Principle
A PE, CPA, or attorney billing at $200–$400/hour does not think about spending $99 on a course. They think about whether the course is worth their 2 hours. Price accordingly — and position accordingly.

Our courses are not cheap. They are efficient and credentialed.

### Anchor Pricing Strategy
- Always show annual subscription value vs. à la carte to make subscription look smart
- Enterprise pricing shown as "per seat" to make large totals more palatable
- Cohort workshops priced as investment (ROI on billable hour savings), not expense

## SALES PIPELINE MANAGEMENT

### Individual Course Funnel
1. LinkedIn post or search → 
2. Lead magnet (checklist) → 
3. Email nurture (5 emails) → 
4. Course page → 
5. Purchase → 
6. Completion → 
7. Upsell to APEX PRO

**Conversion benchmarks:**
- Email opt-in → purchase: 3–8% (aim for 5%)
- Course purchase → APEX PRO upgrade: 15–25%

### Enterprise Funnel
1. Firm decision-maker sees LinkedIn content → connects
2. Direct message: "Would a 30-min call on AI CE for your team be useful?"
3. Discovery call → firm audit → proposal
4. Proposal includes: 1 free pilot course for 5 staff, then upgrade to firm license
5. Close

**Enterprise sales cycle:** 4–12 weeks
**Target close rate from discovery call:** 30%
